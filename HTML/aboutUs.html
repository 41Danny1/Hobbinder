Share. Grow. Inspire.

Hobbiner is a digital solution to this new-found difficulty in finding the right inspiration & hobbies. By offering a convenient platform, it is our goal to help users match with hobbies that are right for them, find people to develop their skills with, and bring to light the hobbies that aren’t as popular as others.

Hobbinder’s target demographic consists of users 18 and older who want to find interests of their own, or have a stage to share their knowledge of their craft. We strive to offer tools and a platform that enables users to build communities of highly-active and engaged members, to foster a healthy collection of regularly user-generated content for various hobbies.
